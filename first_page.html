Beach Photography

Taking pictures is a form of self expression. I enjoy taking pictures of beaches, particularly beaches in Florida (where I'm from). These are some other photographers that do beach photography: Massimo Vitali is an Italian photographer best known for his large-scale color images of beaches. Amber Mozo is an acclaimed surf photographer and the daughter of legendary surf photographer Jon Mozo. Gray Malin is a Los Angeles-based fine-art photographer who does aerial beach photography.
